Welcome to Nusa Dua Luxury Villas

Nusa Dua Luxury Villas are a hallmark of opulence and tranquility in Bali, catering to travelers seeking an unforgettable escape. Nestled within the lush landscapes of Nusa Dua, these villas offer a unique blend of traditional Balinese charm and modern luxury.

Top Attractions

Museum Pasifika

Visiting the Pasifika art collection is one of a time opportunity to relax in an oasis of tranquillity and to view an extraordinary collection of paintings and other artworks.

Geger beach

To go to this beach from our hotel, we used a cab that cost us ten dollars. It was absolutely worth it! The beach has calm, mild water, pristine white sand, sunbathing chairs with umbrellas, and a reasonably priced restaurant.

water blow

Nusa Dua Water Blow is a stunning natural attraction located on the southern coast of Bali, Indonesia. It's famous for its dramatic waves crashing against rugged limestone cliffs, creating spectacular sprays of water that shoot high into the air.
